# Deep Dive: Why Slay the Spire Changed Roguelites Forever

## The Perfect Formula

Slay the Spire nailed three key elements:

1. **Accessible Complexity** - Easy to learn, impossible to master
2. **Build Variety** - Every run feels different
3. **Risk vs. Reward** - Constant meaningful decisions

## The Legacy

Since Slay the Spire, we've seen incredible games follow in its footsteps:

- Griftlands
- And dozens more

## Why It Still Holds Up

Years later, Slay the Spire remains in our S-tier for good reason. The base game is perfectly balanced, and the modding community keeps it fresh with new content.
